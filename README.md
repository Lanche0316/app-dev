# F.R.I.E.N.D.S

## Overview
*F.R.I.E.N.D.S* is an American sitcom that aired from **1994 to 2004**.  
It follows six friends living in New York City as they navigate life, love, and career challenges.

## Main Characters
- **Rachel Green** – Fashion enthusiast, later works at Ralph Lauren  
- **Ross Geller** – Paleontologist, known for his on-again/off-again relationship with Rachel  
- **Monica Geller** – Chef, Ross’s sister, known for her competitiveness and cleanliness  
- **Chandler Bing** – Sarcastic office worker in statistical analysis and data reconfiguration  
- **Joey Tribbiani** – Aspiring actor, famous for his line *“How you doin’?”*  
- **Phoebe Buffay** – Quirky musician, known for her song *“Smelly Cat”*
